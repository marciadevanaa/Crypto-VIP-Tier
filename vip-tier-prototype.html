Design Decisions
Improvements vs PRD
Named tiers not VIP numbers — better HNI appeal. PRD says hide tier numbers anyway.
OR qualification logic made visual — qualify via Volume OR AUM, whichever comes first.
Monthly savings tracker — key retention hook missing from PRD entirely.
Fee breakdown in orders — gross fee, VIP discount, final fee inline at trade time.
"Crypto only" scope is explicitly labeled — critical given Ajaib's multi-product context.
Open Questions
Threshold values (volume/AUM) need final sign-off from Business Ops before going live.
Futures maker/taker fees not yet finalized in PRD — placeholder rates used.
Tier naming (Silver/Gold etc.) needs alignment with Marketing on brand voice.
